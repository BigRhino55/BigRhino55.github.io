A little about me:

When I first started school I had no idea what I wanted to do or what to get my major in. I thought I would do something with engineering but I really didnt like math. So I started looking for what I would be interested in, and I found computers. I took some programming classes and I thought they were awesome, but I had absolutely no idea what I was doing. I had no history with computers other than some of the Microsoft products like word. But I began to catch on to it!

About a year later I started getting interrested in the hardware of computers and really dug into all of the intracate parts of the computer and with the help of my all knowing neighbor I then built my first computer! I bought all of the parts that I meticulously chose and put it all together. It has been the coolest thing I have done with a computer! Then I wanted to see if i could build a computer and see if I could get it running for under $100. This wasn't easy at all because the windows operationg system was already $150, so I needed to find a lot of scraps and deals that would help me. And I am gald to say that I was able to get a computer running for $90! its better than my $600 laptop!

Now I help friends and family with issues on their computer or if they need to make some upgrades. Not only that, I now work at a cyber security buisness that is based in London. It is amazing to look back 3 years ago and to see how much I have learned and grown when it comes to coding and building computers. It really blows my mind because If you told me 3 years ago that I would be where I am now I would run away because it sounds like to much work. But man am I proud of my self! Anything is possible if you put your mind to it and be determined to make any amout of progress daily!
